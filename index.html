Our Services

Yancom Services

1.

DPO as a Service (DPOaaS)

Having access to an experienced and knowledgeable Data Protection Officer as a Service is a cost-effective way to improve information security and ensure compliance with data protection laws like GDPR.

Our DPOaaS offering includes:

✓ Dedicated DPO support with unlimited telephone and email assistance during business hours.
✓ Registration of your DPO with the appropriate supervisory authority.
✓ GDPR gap analysis with a prioritized remedial action plan.
✓ Comprehensive GDPR documentation review, including legal insights.
✓ Guidance on data protection impact assessments (DPIAs), data subject access requests (DSARs), and data breach management.

2.

Data Protection Compliance Audit

Our audit services assess whether your organization has effective policies and procedures in place for managing personal data.

Our comprehensive audit includes:

✓ Assessment of governance, policies, and procedures.
✓ Evaluation of data handling processes for both electronic and manual records.
✓ Review of response procedures for data access requests and third-party sharing.
✓ Analysis of security measures for data protection.
✓ Detailed audit report with actionable recommendations.
